# Retail KPI Business Intelligence Framework

This project simulates a Business Intelligence dashboard using Python.

It demonstrates how raw transactional retail data can be transformed into decision-ready insights through KPI analysis, segmentation, and retention modeling.

---

## Project Objectives

- Compute core retail KPIs
- Analyze revenue trends
- Evaluate customer activity
- Perform cohort-based retention analysis
- Translate analytics into business insights

---

## Key Analytics Components

### Executive KPI Overview
- Total Revenue
- Total Orders
- Total Customers
- Average Order Value (AOV)

### Revenue Trend Analysis
- Monthly revenue tracking
- Month-over-Month (MoM) growth

### Customer Segmentation
- Active
- At Risk
- Lost

### Revenue by Customer Segment
Segment-level revenue and order contribution analysis.

### Geographic Revenue Analysis
Top-performing countries and market concentration insights.

### Cohort Retention Analysis
Customer retention tracking by first purchase month.

---

## Technologies Used

- Python
- Pandas
- NumPy
- Matplotlib

---

## Key Business Insights

- Revenue concentration is driven by a limited number of high-performing countries.
- A small percentage of customers generate a disproportionate share of total revenue.
- Early churn (Month 1) represents the highest retention risk.
- Segment-based targeting can improve marketing efficiency.
